Privacy Policy
----------------

### Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Bad Birdie, a free badminton footwork training app provided by Hong Huynh, respects your privacy. We understand that you care about your data, and we are committed to protecting it.

Bad Birdie does not collect any personal information from users. This means we don't require you to provide your name, email address, location, pictures, or any other data that could be used to identify you.

### Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Bad Birdie saves the following data only on your device, using the standard Android and iOS storage mechanism (SharedPreferences):

- Your training presets (corner selection, sets, shots, speed, rest, shot filter)
- Your last-used training configuration
- Your app preferences (theme, language)

This data never leaves your device and is fully removed when you uninstall the app.

### System Services

Bad Birdie uses the following on-device services. None of them send data to any server:

- **Audio playback** — your device's built-in audio system, used to play the hit sound during training
- **Text-to-speech** — your device's built-in TTS engine, used to announce shot names in speech mode

Both services run entirely locally on your phone.

### Cookies

Bad Birdie does not use cookies. The app does not include a web browser component.

### Third-Party Services

Bad Birdie does not integrate with any third-party services for analytics, advertising, or tracking.

### Security

While Bad Birdie doesn't collect personal data, we still strive to maintain a secure environment by following platform best practices for local data storage.

### Children's Privacy

Bad Birdie is suitable for all ages. We do not target children and don't knowingly collect any information from them.

### Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We recommend reviewing this page for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately upon being posted.
